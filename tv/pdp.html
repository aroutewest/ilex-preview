Procès-verbal de dégustation

Deep garnet in the glass, with plum, black cherry and a trace of cedar on the nose.

The palate is rounded and warm, its tannins settled by the years in oak underground.

The finish holds through a savoury course and pours again without the reckoning of the morning after.
